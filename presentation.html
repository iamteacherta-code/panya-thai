The challenge · ทำไมต้องมีระบบ

Thai reading is complex — memorising isn't enough

  • 44 consonants in 3 classes, 5 tones, clusters, and many irregular spellings
  • Tone depends on consonant class + mark + syllable type — it must be taught explicitly, not guessed
  • Without a system, many learners memorise whole words and stall when texts get harder
  • เด็กจำนวนมากท่องจำเป็นคำ ๆ แทนการถอดรหัส ทำให้อ่านคำใหม่ไม่ได้
Our approach · แนวทาง

Explicit, systematic phonics — built on research

  • UFLI-style scope & sequence — skills build in a careful order, each lesson assumes only what came before
  • Simple View of Reading: Reading = Decoding × Language Comprehension
  • K2–Y3 = “learn to read” (decoding) → Y4–Y6 = “read to learn” (fluency, vocabulary, comprehension, writing)
  • Mastery-based — place students by skill, not by age

What teachers should know · สิ่งที่ครูควรรู้

Designed to drop into your classroom

  • Place by skill, not age — a strong K2 child and a catch-up Y3 child may both be “Beginner”
  • Each lesson = 4 clear parts: objective · what's new · example words · activity
  • Print or project — every worksheet is A4-ready; tools work on the board
  • English grade labels (K2–Y6) so it fits our international grade system
  • Free & shareable — send the link or QR to colleagues, students, parents
How it works · ระบบภายใน

Simple, robust, and free to run

  • A web app — no installation, no login, opens in any browser
  • Hosted free on GitHub Pages; the link is always live
  • Data-driven — one curriculum dataset powers Lessons, Curriculum & Worksheets, so it stays consistent and is easy to update
  • Print-ready A4 output built in; open & maintainable
